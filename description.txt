The Ebonian Mod is a WIP terraria mod that plans on expanding every single biome in the game; currently, only the Crimson, the Corruption and the Underworld have had additions.

Developed by: Ebon, Sten, Max, Yuri O, Truedog, Mjoon, Vade, Roll’eG, Decrypt, Kageby/Dreadsoul, Lunatic, Sulphuric and Taco
